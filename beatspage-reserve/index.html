Feature Demonstration

Paper 2.0 has two main classes of features

There is draw mode, which is still a proof of concept

In the future I want a full toolbar

But before that, I want it to be able to write cleanly over text

There is line mode, which is moderately novel, and most of what will be demonstrated.

In line mode, I've defined three major classes of p tags. Admonitions, swings, and beats

There are, meanwhile, five types of beats that correspond to musical notes.

Eighth beat, quarter beat, half beat, quarter rest, and half rest.

But Paper 2.0's soft features are just as important as its hard features

Paper 2.0 is lightweight

Paper 2.0 is made of eminently simple html, where every line is simply a single "P tag" with a class attached

If you haven't noticed, Paper 2.0 looks just like the looseleaf you used in school

Editing in Paper 2.0 is single click
